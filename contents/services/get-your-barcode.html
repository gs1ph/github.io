Our Organization

GS1 Philippines is a Member Organization (MO) of GS1, a leading global organization dedicated to the design and implementation of global standards and solutions to improve the efficiency and visibility of supply and demand chains globally and across sectors. The GS1 system of standards is the most widely used supply chain standards system in the world.

GS1 Philippines is the only organization internationally recognized and authorized to administer the GS1 System in the Philippines. We are the only body in the Philippines authorized to issue GS1 Company Prefixes (GCP) to be used in composing GS1 barcode numbers.

GS1 Philippines is part of a global barcoding and numbering organization that licenses authorized barcode numbers for businesses. Businesses can then put these barcode numbers on their products to sell them locally and globally. To be licensed to use a GS1 barcode number, one must become a member of a GS1 Member Organization (MO), like GS1 Philippines. Having GS1 Barcodes ensures that each barcode number you are licensed to use is globally unique. No other businesses can use your barcode numbers or barcodes on their products. Major supply and demand chain industries in the Philippines benefit from the efficiency that the GS1 System bring to their businesses.

Rights Certificate

  1. GS1, formerly called 'EAN International', is a voluntary, not-for-profit, international association with a Head Office in Belgium. It has a membership of 101 nationality based Member Organisations. GS1 develops and manages a worlwide, multi-industry system of standards enabling the authomatic identification of products and services in the supply chain and electronic commerce.
  2. GS1 Member Organisations are authorized by GS1 to administer the GS1 System in their own territory and as such are the sole representative of GS1 for that territory. To ensure the global uniqueness of GS1 identifiaction numbers and the integrity of the GS1 System, there can only be one GS1 Member Orgaisation per national territory.
  3. GS1 Philippines has been a Member Organisation of GS1 since 1993. GS1 Philippines is the sole representative of GS1 in the Philippines and has been granted the exclusive right, by GS1, to administer the GS1 System in the Philippines and allocate GS1 identification numbers to companies for their bar-coding and supply chain management needs.
  4. Trademarks. By virtue of its membership in GS1, GS1 Philippines has the exclusive right to use the 'GS1' trademarks in the Philippines.
  5. Copy rights. GS1 Philippines has the exclusive right in the Philippines to copy, translate, reproduce and distribute GS1 publications including the GS1 General Specifications, the GS1 Global User Manual
